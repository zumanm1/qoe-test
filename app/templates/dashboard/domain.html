Transport Domain Information

The TRANSPORT domain consists of two major subdomains:

  • TX_D (Transmission Domain): Connects 2G, 3G, 4G and 5G VRF on PE edge routers towards core networks.
  • CDN_D (Core Data Network Domain): Provides L3VPN services to connect EPC, CS and PS core (4G, LTE and 5G CORE and all related services provisioning).

Note: CDN_D refers to Core Data Network, not Content Delivery Network. It is the department responsible for IP-MPLS network services.
